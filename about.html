Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the simple registration process. It only takes a minute, and you'll immediately gain access to all our features.

What video quality do you offer?

We offer movies in various quality options, including 4K, 1080p, and 720p, depending on your internet connection and device capabilities. All our premium content is available in the highest possible quality.

How do I create a watchlist?

To create a watchlist, simply browse the movies and click the "Add to Watchlist" button on any film you'd like to save for later. You can access and manage your watchlists from your profile page.

Are there any subscription plans?

Yes! We offer flexible subscription plans to suit your needs. You can choose between monthly or annual plans, with discounts for long-term commitments. We also offer a free tier with limited access to our content library.

Can I download movies to watch offline?

Absolutely! Our premium subscribers can download select movies and shows to watch offline on our mobile apps. Downloads are available for 48 hours after you start watching.
